About Showhaus

Showhaus is an open directory for local concerts. Anyone is allowed to submit their shows to the directory so long as they meet our community guidelines.

How does it work?

Showhaus.org gathers its data from both dedicated moderators and a bot that scrapes Facebook pages for public events. We also use the Ticketfly API to collect information about ticketed shows.
