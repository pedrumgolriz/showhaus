About Showhaus

Showhaus is an open-source local show aggregator designed and developed by Luis Queral and Pedrum Golriz.

Aggregation

Shows are aggregated through user submissions and from our list of show feeds.

Feeds are largely user-submitted and comprise of the Facebook pages of bands and venues. Periodocially, Showhaus scrapse these pages for new events and displays them on the site. This method allows us to scape shows not found on traditional ticketing or concert API's.
