About Showhaus

Showhaus is an open classifields for local music.

Aggregation

Shows are aggregated through user submissions and from our list of show feeds.

Feeds are user-submitted and comprise of the Facebook pages of bands and venues. Periodocially, Showhaus scrapse these pages for new events and displays them on the site. This method allows us to scape shows not found on traditional ticketing or concert API's.

Contribute

Showhaus is open-source and we invite anyone to contribute bug fixes and feature enhancements.
