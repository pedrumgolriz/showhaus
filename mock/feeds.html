FEEDS

One of the ways that Showhaus aggregates shows is by scraping Facebook pages for relevant events.

Use the form below to add a feed.

Enter the URL of a Facebook page.
Showhaus will scrape any events associated with that page.

How it Works

For instance, if you run a venue and you add that venue’s Facebook page to our feeds, every public event you create will be added to our list of shows.

Of course, we have the right to remove any feeds that go against our community guidelines. Learn more about that here.
